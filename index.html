Lime Cordiale Fan Site

About

Oli and Louis Leimbach, the originators of Lime Cordiale are from Sydney, Australia. They released their first album in 2017, Permanent Vacation, and have since had great success, landing 5 songs on the triple j's Hottest 100 for 2020, placing them amoungst the likes of Billie Eilish and Violent SoHo. They are also the only act to achieve 5 entries in the top 26 songs and hold the record for the most songs off a studio album to be included in the countdown.
